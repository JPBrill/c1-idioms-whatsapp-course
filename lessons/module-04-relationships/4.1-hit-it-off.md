# Lesson 4.1 — Hit It Off

**Theme:** Relationships & Conflict  
**Level:** C1  
**Idiom:** *hit it off*

---

## 📱 Message 1 — Context & Guess

```
Imagine this:
You meet someone new at a conference. Within ten minutes, you're laughing together, sharing stories, and already planning to meet again next week. You felt an instant connection.

What idiom could describe this immediate chemistry? 🤔
```

---

## 📱 Message 2 — Reveal & Explanation

```
Idiom: "hit it off" 🤝

Meaning: quickly develop a friendly connection with someone — like each other immediately.

Pattern:
• "We hit it off as soon as we met."
• "They really hit it off at the party."
• "I knew we'd hit it off the moment she walked in."

Note: Always used to describe a mutual, positive connection — not one-sided.
```

---

## 📱 Message 3 — Your Turn

```
Your turn! ✍️

1) Write a sentence using "hit it off".

Example: "My flatmates and I hit it off immediately — we're still close friends ten years later."

2) Bonus: Tell us about a time you really hit it off with someone. What made the connection so instant? 🎙️
```

---

## Teacher Notes
- Very natural in both speaking and writing.
- Can be extended: "We really hit it off" or "We hit it off straight away."
