## 📱 Activity 2 — Sentence Transformation

```
🧠 ACTIVITY 2: Rewrite using the idiom

Rewrite each sentence using the correct idiom from this week.

1) "She avoided the main topic for 15 minutes."
→ She ________

2) "He only tells us information when he feels like it — we're never updated."
→ He never ________

3) "I don't completely trust those health claims on the packaging."
→ I take those ________

4) "Can you just tell me the main problem?"
→ Can you just ________

Answers below ⬇️
```

---

## 📱 Activity 2 — Answers

```
✅ SAMPLE ANSWERS:
1) She beat around the bush for 15 minutes.
2) He never keeps us in the loop.
3) I take those health claims with a pinch of salt.
4) Can you just get straight to the point?
```
