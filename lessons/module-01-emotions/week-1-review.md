# Week 1 Review — Emotions & Mood

**Idioms covered:**
- over the moon
- down in the dumps
- on edge
- take something to heart

---

## 📱 Weekly Review Message

```
🔁 WEEK 1 REVIEW — Emotions & Mood

You've learned 4 emotion idioms this week:
✅ over the moon
✅ down in the dumps
✅ on edge
✅ take something to heart

Here's your challenge 👇
```

---

## 📱 Activity 1 — Mini Story

```
✍️ ACTIVITY 1: Mini Story

Write a short paragraph (4–6 sentences) using ALL FOUR idioms from this week.

The paragraph must tell a story that makes sense.
You can use any situation — real or imaginary.

Example opening: "Last week was an emotional rollercoaster..."

Post your paragraph here when you're ready! 👇
```

---

## 📱 Activity 2 — Fill the Gap Quiz

```
🧠 ACTIVITY 2: Fill the Gap

Choose the correct idiom for each situation:

1) She just found out she got the job. She called her mum and said: "Mum, I'm ________!"

2) He failed his exam and hasn't spoken to anyone since. He's clearly ________.

3) It's 10 minutes before the presentation. She's checking her notes every 30 seconds. She's completely ________.

4) The teacher said his writing was "a bit flat". He hasn't smiled since. He really ________ the comment ________.

Answers in the next message! ⬇️
```

---

## 📱 Activity 2 — Answers

```
✅ ANSWERS:

1) over the moon
2) down in the dumps
3) on edge
4) took ... to heart

How many did you get right? Reply with your score! 🎯
```

## Teacher Notes
- The mini story task is ideal as a voice note challenge.
