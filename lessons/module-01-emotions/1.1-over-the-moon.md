# Lesson 1.1 — Over the Moon

**Theme:** Emotions & Mood  
**Level:** C1  
**Idiom:** *over the moon*

---

## 📱 Message 1 — Context & Guess

```
Imagine this:
You finally get the IELTS score you need. You open the email and you feel extremely happy — almost like you are floating.

What idiom could you use to describe this feeling? 🤔

(Reply before reading on!)
```

---

## 📱 Message 2 — Reveal & Explanation

```
Native speakers often say: "I'm over the moon." 🌙

Meaning: extremely happy and excited — much stronger than just "happy".

Pattern:
• "I was over the moon when ..."
• "She's over the moon about ..."
• "We were absolutely over the moon."

Note: You can add "absolutely" or "completely" to sound even more natural.
```

---

## 📱 Message 3 — Your Turn

```
Your turn! ✍️

1) Write one sentence about a time YOU were over the moon.

Example: "I was over the moon when I finally finished my thesis."

2) Bonus: Record a short voice note using it in a mini-story. 🎙️
```

---

## Teacher Notes
- Common mistake: students say "I am on the moon" — remind them it's *over* the moon.
- Encourage exaggerated tone in voice notes to practise natural emphasis.
