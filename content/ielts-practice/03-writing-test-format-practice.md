## Exercise 4: Scoring Criteria Matching

Match each examiner comment (1–4) to the scoring criterion it relates to (A–D).

**Examiner comments:**
1. "The candidate uses a range of complex sentence structures with only minor errors."
2. "Ideas are well-organised into paragraphs with clear topic sentences and logical linking."
3. "The candidate addresses all parts of the question and presents a clear position throughout."
4. "A wide range of vocabulary is used appropriately, including less common items and collocations."

**Criteria:**
- A. Task Achievement / Response
- B. Coherence & Cohesion
- C. Lexical Resource
- D. Grammatical Range & Accuracy
